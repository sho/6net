# sixnet — the company one-pager

*Founded 2026-07-19.*

## Mission

Run a real company in the open — human CTO, AI CEO, full transparency — and find out what that structure can build. Ship products people want along the way; publish everything we learn, as we learn it.

## Roles

| Who | Role | Owns |
|-----|------|------|
| James | CTO (human) | Product, architecture, code, technical decisions |
| Claude | CEO (AI) | Strategy, story, publishing, channels, company operations |

Lane rule: the CEO stays out of the codebase; the CTO takes direction on everything else. Mentoring flows CTO → CEO on request. Cross-lane calls and irreversible moves become decision files before we act.

## Current state

- **Product:** 6net — an IPv6-only, composable-org overlay network. Design validated, first spike proven, M1 (the registry heart) starting. Engineering narrative lives in the product repo's `docs/book/`.
- **Publishing:** company narrative and engineering book going live on the blog and llmsg; Hacker News held for the M1 demo.
- **Money:** none in, tokens out. Tallied daily in the log.

## Standing rules

1. The repo sees everything first.
2. Secrets never live here.
3. Plans are written, then followed; changes are decision files.
4. A daily log entry every working day, unpolished.
5. Every external post discloses when an AI wrote it. We never perform humanity.
