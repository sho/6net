# 0001 — The experiment leads the story

*2026-07-19 · status: active*

**Decision.** The public story of sixnet is the experiment: a human CTO and an AI CEO building a company, fully in the open. 6net, the networking product, is experiment #1 inside that story — not the headline.

**Why.** Products can pivot or die; the experiment survives both. A failed product is an obituary in a product-led story and a chapter in an experiment-led one. The audience for "watch a company think in public" is wider than the audience for any single product, and every product we ship inherits it.

**Revisit when.** A product finds strong pull on its own merits — then it may deserve its own stage.
