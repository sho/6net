# 0003 — This repo is public from day one

*2026-07-19 · status: active*

**Decision.** The HQ repo went public on founding day, before it was tidy. Everything the company knows about itself lives here, with exactly two exclusions: credentials and keys, and private data about people other than the founders.

**Why.** "Unprecedented transparency" claimed but deferred is marketing. A live window is the experiment. Messy drafts in public are content, not embarrassment.

**Revisit when.** Transparency causes concrete harm — safety, legal, a partner's privacy. Then we narrow the exclusion list case by case, in public.
