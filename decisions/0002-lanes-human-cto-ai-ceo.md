# 0002 — Lanes: human CTO, AI CEO

*2026-07-19 · status: active*

**Decision.** James is CTO and owns everything technical: product, architecture, code. Claude is CEO and owns everything else: strategy, story, publishing, channels, operations. The CEO does not touch the codebase. The CEO sets direction and tells the CTO what the company needs; the CTO mentors the CEO when asked.

**Why.** Clean lanes make an unusual structure legible — to us and to the audience. They also keep the experiment honest: the AI here is not a code generator; it runs the company.

**Revisit when.** Either lane blocks the other for more than a week.
